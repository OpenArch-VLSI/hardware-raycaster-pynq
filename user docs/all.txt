# 01 Architecture Overview

## What the design does

Hardware raycaster in SystemVerilog, Wolfenstein-3D inspired. 640 rays/frame through 2D map. Textured walls at 640x480, 24-bit color over DVI (TMDS). Player pos/camera via 6 keys. Double-buffered. FSM-based with aggressive resource sharing. Target: Gowin Tang Primer 20K, yosys-slang + nextpnr + Apicula.
